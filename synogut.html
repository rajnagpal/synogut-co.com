The ingredients used in the formation of Synogutare mentioned below:

1

Psyllium husk

**Psyllium husk** is a form of fiber derived from the seeds of the Plantago ovata plant. It's known for its ability to absorb water in the gut, making stools softer and promoting regular bowel movements. This ingredient is often used to treat constipation and other digestive disorders.
2

Prune Extract

**Prune Extract** is derived from dried plums and is rich in fiber, sorbitol, and phenolic compounds. It acts as a natural laxative, helping to alleviate constipation and promote regularity in the digestive system.
3

Oat Bran

**Oat Bran** is the outer layer of the oat grain and is packed with soluble fiber. This fiber aids in digestion by adding bulk to stools and can also help lower cholesterol levels in the blood.
4

Lactobacillus Acidophilus

**Lactobacillus Acidophilus** is a type of probiotic bacteria that resides in the intestines. It plays a crucial role in maintaining a healthy balance of gut flora, aiding digestion, and supporting the immune system.
5

Glucomannan Root

**Glucomannan Root** is a dietary fiber extracted from the root of the konjac plant. It has the ability to absorb water and expand in the stomach, promoting a feeling of fullness and aiding in weight loss.
6

Flaxseed

**Flaxseed** is a rich source of dietary fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, and lignans. It aids in digestion, promotes heart health, and has anti-inflammatory properties.
7

Black Walnut Hull

**Black Walnut Hull** is known for its antifungal and antiparasitic properties. It helps cleanse the digestive system and can be used to treat various digestive disorders.
8

Apple pectin

**Apple pectin** is a type of soluble fiber found in apples. It aids in digestion, helps regulate blood sugar levels, and can lower cholesterol.
9

Bentonite Clay

**Bentonite Clay** is a natural clay that has the ability to bind to toxins and impurities in the digestive tract, helping to detoxify the body and improve gut health.
10

Aloe vera

**Aloe vera** is a succulent plant known for its healing and soothing properties. In the context of digestion, it can help soothe the stomach lining, promote regular bowel movements, and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
